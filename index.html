Science Flash Cards

Welcome to Science Flash Cards!

This interactive flashcard game is designed to help students review and test their knowledge of key science concepts in Physics, Biology, and Chemistry. It's perfect for exam preparation or as a fun learning activity.

How to Play:

  1. Choose a science topic using the navigation buttons at the top.
  2. Each flashcard shows a keyword. Match it with the correct definition from the dropdown options below.
  3. Use the "Hint" button for help if you're stuck (you have two hints per topic).
  4. The circular timer shows how much time you have to complete the set. If it runs out, the correct answers will be revealed.
  5. Click "Check Answers" to see how you did. Your progress will be tracked on the scoreboard.
